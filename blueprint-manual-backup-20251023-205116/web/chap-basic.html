2 Basic Definitions

Definition 2.1 Manifold Type

blueRicciFlow.Basic green\(\checkmark \)We work with a type \(M\) equipped with a topological space structure and a charted space structure modeled on \(\mathbb {R}\).

Lemma 2.2 Positive multiplication

blueRicciFlow.pos_mul_pos green\(\checkmark \) The product of two positive real numbers is positive.

Lemma 2.3 Square positivity

blueRicciFlow.square_pos_of_ne_zero green\(\checkmark \)The square of a nonzero real number is strictly positive.

Lemma 2.4 Existence of positive real

blueRicciFlow.exists_pos_real green\(\checkmark \)There exists a positive real number.

Lemma 2.5 Inverse positivity

blueRicciFlow.inv_pos_of_pos green\(\checkmark \)The inverse of a positive real number is positive.

Lemma 2.6 Continuous at characterization

blueRicciFlow.continuousAt_iff_continuousWithinAt green\(\checkmark \) A function is continuous at a point if and only if it is continuous within the universal set at that point.
